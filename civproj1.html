Client Name: U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Risk Management Agency (RMA)

Project: Mission Support IT Services, RMA IV

Dollar Value: $156 Million

FEDSIM’s partnership with RMA spans more than 15 years, successfully supporting RMA’s mission to serve America’s agricultural producers through effective, market-based risk management tools and solutions to strengthen the economic stability of agricultural producers and rural communities. RMA operates and manages the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC), and reinsures risk management products sold and serviced by private sector insurance companies. RMA required the full range of IT services to develop and deliver new crop insurance products, sustain high employee productivity, build and maintain new IT systems, and design, deploy, and maintain the underlying infrastructure for RMA systems. Through the collaborative partnership, FEDSIM provided acquisition excellence by awarding a task order using the GSA ALLIANT Large Government Wide Acquisition Contract (GWAC).
