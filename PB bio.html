Pete Burr | Deputy Director

As the Deputy Director for FEDSIM, Pete manages the day-to-day operations of the organization, ensuring that each FEDSIM client receives continuous high quality acquisition and program management support across each contract’s lifecycle. Pete brings nearly 30 years of acquisition and program management experience within the government and industry to FEDSIM.

Prior to his current position, Pete led the FEDSIM Civilian Sector supporting Civilian Federal agencies in the acquisition and management of Information Technology and Professional Service solutions. He also brings experience from his time with the Department of Health and Human Services and NASA’s Goddard Space Flight Center.

Pete resides in Shady Side, Maryland with his wife Elizabeth, his daughter Emily and step children Maddie and Talbott.
