Frequently Asked Questions


WHAT ARE THE TYPICAL PROJECTS FEDSIM SUPPORTS?

FEDSIM focuses on complex acquisitions, requiring highly-trained acquisition and program management staff with a deep understanding of conducting IT and Professional Services FAR based evaluations.

WILL YOU PROVIDE/ASSIGN A CONTRACTING OFFICER’S REPRESENTATIVE (COR) IF ONE IS NOT AVAILABLE BY THE REQUESTING AGENCY?

Yes. FEDSIM will provide/assign a COR as part of our service.

WILL YOU WRITE THE SOW/SOO/PWS FOR THE REQUESTING AGENCY?

FEDSIM’s Acquisition Project Manager and Project Manager will work with the requesting agency to write the SOW/SOO/PWS as part of our service.

IS THE REQUESTING AGENCY RESPONSIBLE FOR PROVIDING TECHNICAL EVALUATION TEAM?

Yes. The requesting agency is responsible for designating the Technical Evaluation Team.

IS THERE A FEE ASSOCIATED WITH THE ASSISTED ACQUISITION SERVICES?

Yes. The assisted acquisition fee is based on each individual acquisition.
