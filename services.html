Our Services

FEDSIM offers acquisition and program management expertise to deliver the best value and innovative acquisition solutions to support the entire federal government.

 
Acquisition
Management
We offer continuons agile acquisiton management, ensuring that our clients can keep up with rapidly changing technologies and operations.
Financial
Management
We track and manage client funding and oversee the invoicing process.
Project
Management
We provide project manangement services across the life cycle of the contract, from pre-award requirements gathering to post-award administration, to contract closeout.
 
IT Services
 
We offer acquisition support to buy IT services (such as cybersecurity, application development, and IT infrastructure) to support each client's needs.
Professional
Services
We help acquire professional services from contractor resources (such as technical training, language services, and marketing services).
Managed
Services
We provide facilities maintenance acquisition through contractor resources, ensuring seamless integration of our client's people, place, process, and technology.
