Rob Coen | Strategy Director

Rob is seasoned government program and acquisitions professional with over 20 years of experience in the federal environment. As the Director of Strategy for FEDSIM, Rob is responsible for managing strategic partnerships with federal customer agencies and overseeing FEDSIMâ€™s expansion efforts in both the civilian and defense sectors.

Prior to joining GSA, Mr. Coen served as the director of the NIH Information Technology Acquisition and Assessment Center (NITAAC), where he oversaw a team of sixty-five individuals dedicated to managing three GWAC contracts (CIO-SP3, CIO-SP3sb and CIO-CS) and an assisted acquisition program. During his tenure, Rob was responsible for transforming the NITAAC offerings from a single-service line of business to full-service strategic program dedicated to customer service, quality, outreach, and business development - a move that brought NITAAC from under $100 million to over $5 billion annually.

Rob holds FAC-C III and FAC COR II certifications. He is involved in numerous professional organizations, serving on the Board of Advisors for NCMA as well as an ACT/IAC fellow and 2012 Partner Program graduate.
