Client: U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS)

Project: Continuous Diagnostics and Monitoring (CDM)

Dollar Value: $6 Billon ceiling

CDM provides the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), federal departments and agencies, and state, local, regional, and tribal governments with tools and services that automate continuous network monitoring; correlate and analyze security-related information; and enhance risk-based decision making at the agency and federal enterprise level. CDM covers 15 continuous monitoring capabilities at every level of the network -- not just the periphery -- allowing agencies to see the effectiveness of their cybersecurity systems. The CDM portfolio consists of 17 blanket purchase agreements (BPAs) awarded off the GSA schedule 70 vehicle.
