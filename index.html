Value-added solutions

FEDSIM delivers innovative solutions to the most challenging acquisitions.

Acquisition Management

We offer continuous agile acquisition management, ensuring that our clients can keep up with rapidly changing technologies and operations.

Financial Management

We track and manage client funding and oversee the invoicing process.

Project Management

We provide project management services across the life cycle of the contract, from pre-award requirements gathering to post-award administration, to contract closeout.

IT Services
