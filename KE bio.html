Ken Evans | DoD/Air Force Sector Director

Kenneth R. Evans is the Director for the DoD/Air Force Sector for FEDSIM. Within this capacity, Ken is responsible for overseeing the procurement and management of task orders supporting over 45 different DoD organizations including Joint Improvised Explosive Device Defeat Office (JIEDDO), U.S. Central Command, Army Human Resource Command, Joint Strike Fighter, and the NAVAIR Rapid Response Program Office.
